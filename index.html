Comprehensive homecare services tailored to your needs

LivingRite Care offers a full range of home-based healthcare services designed to support recovery, independence, and quality of life.

Critical nursing & medical care

Hospital-level nursing delivered at home by licensed, professionally trained nurses and doctors.

  • Post-ICU & post-surgery recovery
  • Medication administration & monitoring
  • Wound care and vital sign checks
  • 24/7 nursing support when required

Stroke rehab & chronic care

Specialist support to help patients regain independence and manage long-term conditions at home.

  • Stroke rehabilitation programmes
  • Chronic disease management
  • Physiotherapy at home
  • Nutrition and dietary support

Elderly, palliative & family support

Compassionate care that helps seniors age gracefully at home while supporting families every step of the way.

  • Geriatric & elderly care
  • Palliative & end-of-life care
  • Companionship and daily living support
  • Guidance and education for family members
